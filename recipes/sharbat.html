Sharbat

Description

Ingredients

  • 4 sīrs of potherb juice
  • 1 sīr of lime juice
  • 1/2 a tūlcha of saffron
  • 1/4 of a māsa each of:
    • nutmeg
    • cinnamon
    • spikenard
    • cloves
    • tagrī
  • 2 rattīs of musk
  • 2 rattīs of camphor
  • 1 māsa of white ambergris

Method

  1. Grind and sift the specified measures of nutmeg, cinnamon, spikenard, cloves, tagri, musk, camphor, and white ambergris. Set aside.
  2. Combine the potherb juice and the lime juice and "cook...slowly over the fire until soft" and "thickened."
  3. Add the nutmeg, cinnamon, spikenard, cloves, and tagri to the juice. Stir.
  4. Add the musk, camphor, and white ambergris. Stir.
  5. Once the mixture has come together, "[k]eep the sherbet in a stone receptacle and drink it whenever it is required."
